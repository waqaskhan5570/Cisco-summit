Session:

Collaboration Flex Plan – The Best way to buy meetings, team collaboration, calling, and contact center

Profile: Fayroz Mehdi joined Cisco via the WebEx acquisition back in 2007 which gave him early exposure to Cloud collaboration as technology area and SaaS as a business model. In his most recent role he has been driving both on-prem and cloud collaboration business in Qatar with additional focus on enabling partners across Gulf. Fayroz holds a Bachelor of Commerce degree from Bangalore University and in his free time loves to hone his skills in his new found love for cooking.
